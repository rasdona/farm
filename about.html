About AgriConnect Nepal

Building bridges between Nepal's agricultural communities through technology, trust, and teamwork.

Our Mission

AgriConnect Nepal was founded with a simple mission: to make it easier for farmers to find reliable workers and for agricultural workers to find meaningful employment. We believe that technology can transform Nepal's agricultural sector by connecting people efficiently and transparently.

Our platform serves all 77 districts of Nepal, supporting local languages and understanding the unique needs of rural agricultural communities.
